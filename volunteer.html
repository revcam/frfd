Join Our Team

Make a difference in your community — become a volunteer with Filer Rural Fire District.

We're Hiring Volunteers

Filer Rural Fire District is actively seeking dedicated individuals to join our volunteer team. Whether you're interested in fire suppression, emergency medical services, or community education, there's a place for you here.

No prior experience is required. We provide all the training you need to serve safely and effectively.

What to Expect

  • Comprehensive fire suppression training
  • Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) certification opportunities
  • Ongoing professional development and continuing education
  • Access to modern equipment and apparatus
  • A team environment built on trust and mutual support

Minimum Requirements

  • 18 years of age or older
  • Valid Idaho driver's license
  • Ability to pass a background check
  • Commitment to training and response obligations
  • Reside or work within a reasonable distance of the district

Time Commitment

Volunteers are expected to attend regular training sessions and respond to emergency calls when available. We understand our members have full-time jobs and families — we work with your schedule.
