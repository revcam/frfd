Filer Rural Fire District's EMS division is pleased to announce the addition of a new Quick Response Unit (QRU) to our fleet. The QRU allows our trained EMTs to reach medical emergencies faster, providing life-saving care while full ambulance resources are coordinated.

This addition reflects the district's ongoing commitment to improving emergency medical services for everyone in our coverage area.
