title: July 4th 2025 Tanker Standby
date: "2025-07-04"
excerpt: District crews were on standby with a tanker unit throughout the July 4th holiday weekend to protect the community during elevated fire risk conditions.
image: ""
---

With dry conditions and fireworks activity across the district, Filer Rural Fire District deployed a tanker unit on standby throughout the July 4th holiday weekend. Crews remained ready to respond quickly to any fire starts in the area.

We remind all residents to use fireworks responsibly and to follow all local ordinances. If you see a fire, call 911 immediately.
