Filer Rural Fire District was proud to host a traditional push-in ceremony in August 2025, welcoming a new piece of apparatus into service. The push-in ceremony is a long-standing fire service tradition where community members and fellow firefighters physically push the new truck into the station for the first time — symbolizing the community's support for its fire department.

We are grateful for the continued support of our community and our partner agencies who joined us for this milestone.
