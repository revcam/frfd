Accessibility Statement

Filer Rural Fire District is committed to ensuring this website is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.

Our Commitment

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. If you experience any accessibility barriers on this site, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Contact Us

To report an accessibility issue or request content in an alternative format, contact us at (208) 326-4111 or use our contact form.
